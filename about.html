I'm Alessandro Sirio Ceresa (b. 2003), an Italian freelance artist and visual designer.

I'm currently completing my studies in New Technologies of Art at the Albertina Academy of Fine Arts in Turin, following a background in graphic design.

My practice moves between visual art and experimental design processes, with a particular focus on semi-realistic imagery and oil painting. I am interested in how traditional techniques and digital approaches can intersect, shaping hybrid visual languages, but I'm also openly against the current GenAI trends.

Fantasy visual culture, role-playing games, and videogames play a significant role in my work, often influencing both the aesthetic and narrative dimensions of my projects.

Since 2021, I have been an active volunteer with the Italian Red Cross, where I have also contributed to the visual identity of my local committee.

I openly identify as queer and maintain a strong interest in contemporary sociopolitical dynamics.
