The Problem

The original identity for Unseen Cat Studio struggled with visibility and clarity. While the dark, atmospheric logo aligned conceptually with the studio’s focus on psychological horror, it relied too heavily on low contrast and subtle details.

The Idea

The redesign builds on duality—comfort versus unease, seen versus unseen.

The Solution

High-contrast palette, simplified silhouette, improved readability and scalability.
