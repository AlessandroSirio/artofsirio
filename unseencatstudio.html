The Problem

Unseen Cat Studio needed a visual identity capable of representing the psychological and emotional tone of its games without relying on generic horror aesthetics.

The original logo already communicated darkness and mystery through the silhouette of a black cat with glowing eyes, but it lacked flexibility, clarity, and symbolic depth when applied across different media.

The challenge was to redesign the identity while preserving the studio’s unsettling atmosphere and emotional core. The logo had to feel minimal yet recognizable, able to visually express the duality at the center of the studio’s work: comfort and fear, familiarity and unease, childhood memories and distorted perception.

The Idea

The redesign started from the concept of duality.

Since the studio’s narratives explore contrasting emotional states, I wanted the identity itself to visually embody that tension.

The cat became the central symbolic element — not simply as a mascot, but as a metaphor for ambiguity and emotional contrast.

I explored mirrored silhouettes, monochromatic compositions, and simplified forms inspired by editorial minimalism and contemporary indie game branding.

The goal was to create something clean and immediate while still maintaining an uncanny feeling beneath the surface: a logo that appears calm at first glance, but subtly unsettling the longer you look at it.

The Solution

The final identity introduces a sharper and more modular visual system centered around two contrasting cat silhouettes.

The black-and-white composition reinforces the idea of duality while improving readability, scalability, and adaptability across digital and printed applications.

The typography was redesigned to feel more structured and contemporary, balancing geometric precision with the organic shape of the symbol. The result is a visual identity that feels atmospheric, recognizable, and emotionally coherent with the studio’s narrative direction.

The redesign successfully transforms the logo into a stronger symbolic mark — one capable of representing both the emotional depth and the unsettling tone of Unseen Cat Studio.
